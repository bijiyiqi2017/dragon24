Welcome to the Dragon Fruit Delight Word Scramble Game!

Unscramble the letters to form a word.

Level 1: Fruits

How to Play:

  1. A scrambled word will appear on the screen.
  2. Type the unscrambled word into the text field.
  3. Click the "Check Answer" button.
  4. If your answer is correct, you'll see a success message, and a new scrambled word will appear.
  5. If your answer is incorrect, you'll see a message asking you to try again.
